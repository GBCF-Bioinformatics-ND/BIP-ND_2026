# Code of Conduct

This workshop adopts [The Carpentries Code of Conduct](https://docs.carpentries.org/policies/coc/) in full. The short version:

We are dedicated to providing a welcoming and supportive environment for all people, regardless of background or identity. We do not tolerate harassment of participants in any form. Behavior that excludes, demeans, or harasses any participant — by speech, conduct, or any other means — will be addressed directly by the instructors and may result in removal from the workshop.

This applies to all workshop venues: the room, the etherpad, Slack/Zoom chat, social events, and any communication channel created for the workshop.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this code, contact any instructor in person, by Slack DM, or by email. Reports are confidential. We will take action.

## Scope

The code applies for the duration of the workshop and to all participants — learners, helpers, instructors, and observers.
